Sprint Three

Sprint three brief

In this sprint I made a lot of progress, I made an edit order function, where the user can delete the order by changing the amount to zero or they can reduce and increase the amount. I also made a Review order function, they can review their total order. With validation, I made sure they couldn't review their order if they havent order anything. The system tells them "you havent ordered anything yet, please order first". I also added a customer info function, here the user enters their name, address and phone number if they choose their order to be delivered. If they choose pick up, they enter their name, phone number and the tells them how long it will take for their meal to be made. Each meal takes 20mins to make, therefore I multiply 20mins by the different type of dishes in their order list. I added validation here which prevents them from reviewing their order if they haven't ordered anything. If the customer has aready entered their details and they enter the customer details option again, then the system says "We already have your details, would you like to re-enter your them (Y/N)". If they select Y, then the list clears and they have re-enter their details and they can even change whether they want pick up or delivery. I also did a little bit of tidying up, such as editing the format of my program (e.g lines etc.)
